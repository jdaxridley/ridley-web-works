FAQ

Common questions

Answers to what most people ask before getting started.

Do I need to provide photos?

No. We use professional visuals that match your industry. If you have job-site photos or truck photos you want included, send them and we'll use those instead.

What if I don't have a logo?

No problem. We design a clean text logo for you as part of every build. If you want a full custom logo with an icon, that's included in the Premium plan.

Can I keep my existing domain?

Yes. If you already own a domain, we'll point it to your new site. If you don't have one, we'll walk you through buying one — it typically runs $10-15/year.

What do I actually need to provide?

Business name, phone number, list of services, and the city you serve. That's the minimum. The more you give us, the more personalized the site — but we can work with just the basics.

What if I want changes after it's live?

On a monthly plan, small edits are included — just text us. If you bought outright, updates are $50 each or you can switch to a subscription anytime.

How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?

We build it for you. You never touch a template editor. The site is designed specifically for your trade and city — not a generic layout you drag and drop yourself. And we're available when something needs changing.
