FAQ

Common questions

Answers to what most people ask before getting started.

Do I need to provide photos?

No. We use professional stock visuals matched to your industry. If you have job-site or truck photos you want included, send them and we'll use those instead.

What if I don't have a logo?

No problem. We design a clean text logo for you as part of every build. Full custom logo with an icon is available on higher plans.

Can I keep my existing domain?

Yes. If you already own a domain, we'll point it to your new site. If you don't have one, we'll walk you through buying one — typically $10–15/year.

What do I actually need to provide?

Business name, phone number, list of services, and the city you serve. That's the minimum. The more you give us, the more personalized the site.

How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?

We build it for you. You never touch a template editor. The site is designed specifically for your trade and city — not a generic layout you drag and drop yourself.
