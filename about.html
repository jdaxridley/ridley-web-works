Built by someone who gets it

Not an agency. Not a template mill. One developer who builds great websites fast, hosts them, and keeps them running.

My name is Dax Ridley. I'm a web developer based in Kansas City. I've been building websites since I was a teenager, learning by doing — taking on real projects, solving real problems, and getting better with every one.

I started paying attention to local service businesses and noticed the same problem everywhere. The ones losing customers had either no website at all, or a DIY template that looked like it was built in 2014. The ones winning had professional sites with booking, reviews, and a way for customers to reach them instantly.

The gap wasn't talent or quality of work. It was a website. Agencies were charging $3,000 to $5,000 upfront plus $500 or more per month just to keep the lights on. For a small business running on tight margins, that's not realistic. There's a better way.

So I built a system. I use purpose-built tools for each industry to deliver custom sites in 48 hours — with booking calendar, contact forms, and everything else included. Same quality a $5K agency delivers, at a price that actually makes sense.

I build every site myself. You talk to me, not an account manager. When you text at 9pm asking to change a phone number, I'm the one who does it.

I build your site the same way I'd build it if my own business depended on it. Because my reputation does.
